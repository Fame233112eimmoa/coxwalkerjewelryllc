Privacy Policy

How Cox Walker Jewelry LLC collects, uses, and protects information.

This policy explains how information may be collected through the website, enquiries, store appointments, and business communications. It is designed to give visitors a clear view of what data is used for and how requests can be managed.

Information We Collect

We may collect names, email addresses, telephone numbers, appointment requests, investor enquiries, product interests, and message content voluntarily submitted through forms or direct communications.

How Information Is Used

Submitted information may be used to respond to enquiries, schedule appointments, provide product or business materials, improve service quality, and maintain orderly records of client and partner communications.

Sharing and Disclosure

Cox Walker Jewelry LLC does not sell personal information. Data may be shared with internal teams, trusted service providers, or legal and compliance advisers only when needed to support legitimate business operations or legal obligations.

Retention and Security

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for service, recordkeeping, compliance, or dispute management. We aim to maintain practical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information and the context of the relationship.

Your Choices and Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of submitted information where applicable. You may also ask us to stop using your contact details for non-essential follow-up communication.

Contact About Privacy

Privacy-related requests can be directed through our contact page or by email at info@coxwalkerjewelryllc.com. We review privacy requests carefully and respond as reasonably practicable.
