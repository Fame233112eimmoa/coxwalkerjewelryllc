Our Story

Founded in 1996, carried from one generation of the Cox family to the next.

The story of Cox Walker Jewelry LLC begins with Kenneth Cox Walker, an English-born entrepreneur who relocated to the United States and founded the business in 1996. What began as a determined family venture has grown into a premium gemstone and jewelry company now led by his daughter, Maria Cox Walker, after Kenneth’s passing in 2019.

1996

Foundation

Kenneth Cox Walker founded Cox Walker Jewelry LLC in the United States in 1996 after leaving England to pursue a larger business vision. He believed there was room for a gemstone company that would not only trade beautiful stones, but also build a reputation around provenance, trust, family name, and the quiet discipline of doing business properly.

2001

Family Foundations

As the company matured, Robert Cox and Mavis Cox became more deeply involved in supporting the family enterprise. Robert helped reinforce sourcing relationships, operational thinking, and practical commercial decision-making, while Mavis helped preserve the service culture, responsibility, and consistency that gave the company its family-led character.

2010

Mine-to-Market Growth

The business expanded beyond simple sourcing into a broader model that connected exploration support, gemstone handling, preparation, cutting, jewelry manufacturing, and private presentation. Kenneth wanted the company to control more of the journey so clients could understand not just the beauty of a stone, but also the story, care, and accountability behind it.

2019

Loss, Succession, and Responsibility

Kenneth Cox Walker passed away in 2019, a moment that marked both profound family loss and a defining leadership transition for the company. His daughter, Maria Cox Walker, as his next of kin, stepped forward to continue the work he began, taking on the responsibility of protecting both the company and the family name her father had built with such care after making a new life in the United States.

Today

Maria Cox Walker’s Chapter

Under Maria’s leadership, the company continues to develop as a modern premium gemstone and jewelry house while remaining deeply rooted in its family history. Working alongside Robert Cox and Mavis Cox, she carries forward Kenneth’s belief that rarity must be matched by integrity, and that the strongest luxury businesses are built not only on product, but on memory, stewardship, and trust passed from one generation to the next.
