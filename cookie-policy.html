Cookie Policy

How small data files may be used to support a smoother website experience.

Cookies and similar technologies can help a site remember preferences, improve performance, and understand how pages are being used. This policy explains those functions in a simple, plain-language format.

Essential Cookies

Essential cookies may support core features such as navigation, accessibility preferences, and basic security functions that help the site operate reliably.

Performance and Analytics

Performance-related cookies may be used to understand page visits, content patterns, and technical behavior so the website can be improved over time.

Preference Storage

Some cookies may remember visitor choices such as interface preferences or previously submitted interactions where this is helpful to the browsing experience.

Managing Cookies

Visitors can usually manage or delete cookies through their browser settings. Restricting cookies may affect the way some site functions behave, especially where convenience features depend on stored preferences.

Third-Party Services

If third-party tools are connected in the future for analytics, maps, forms, or embedded services, those providers may also use cookies under their own policies and settings.

Questions

If you have questions about cookie use, you can contact Cox Walker Jewelry LLC through the main contact page for further clarification.
