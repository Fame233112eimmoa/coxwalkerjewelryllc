Equipment & Machinery

Built around reliable movement, handling, and material control.

Each machine supports a practical, traceable workflow from extraction to preliminary sorting and plant intake. Equipment is selected not only for power, but for reliability, maintainability, and its ability to support safer and more predictable operations.

Excavators

Bench shaping and controlled recovery supported by accurate positioning, measured digging depth, and stable handling.

Drilling Rigs

Access preparation and exploration support for survey programs, sampling campaigns, and targeted site development.

Haul Trucks

Material transfer between the pit and plant with dependable payload movement and controlled routing.

Crushers

Measured reduction for consistent downstream handling, smoother plant intake, and more predictable sorting behavior.

Sorting Lines

Clean inspection stages before grading and transfer, helping preserve organization, accountability, and visual control.
