Our Stores

Our North American store network, currently streamlined to one active location.

Cox Walker Jewelry LLC maintains a select retail and private salon footprint across Florida, Atlanta, and Canada. At present, most locations are marked temporarily closed while the network is being reviewed and refined, with one flagship store still serving clients by appointment.

Temporary Retail Update

Our store program has been designed around private service, appointment-led selling, and a quieter luxury experience across North America. In this phase of the business, the company is consolidating several salons while reviewing location strategy, client flow, and future reopening opportunities.

Five listed stores are currently marked temporarily closed. The Toronto flagship remains active and available for private consultations, gemstone viewings, trade appointments, and client meetings coordinated directly through our team.

For the most accurate access information, appointment support, or future reopening notices, clients are encouraged to contact our concierge team before visiting any location listed below.
