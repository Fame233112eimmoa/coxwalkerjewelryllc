FAQ

Answers to common questions about sourcing, jewelry, appointments, and trade support.

This page is designed to answer the most common client, trade, and investor questions in one clear place. If you need something more specific, our team can guide you directly through private appointments and tailored support.

What does Cox Walker Jewelry LLC specialize in?

Cox Walker Jewelry LLC operates across gemstones, diamonds, and fine jewelry with a mine-to-market perspective. Our focus spans sourcing, processing, cutting, private presentation, bespoke jewelry development, and selective commercial sales.

Which gemstones are featured by the company?

Our site highlights diamonds, emeralds, rubies, sapphires, tanzanite, aquamarine, opals, tourmaline, and morganite. These categories are presented across the gemstones, products, and jewelry collection pages for easier comparison.

Can I book a private appointment to view jewelry or stones?

Yes. Private appointments can be requested through the contact page, and the Toronto flagship is currently the primary active location receiving scheduled visits. Appointments help us prepare the right inventory, documents, and presentation team for your enquiry.

Do your diamonds and gemstones come with certification?

Certification support depends on the product category and the commercial structure of the enquiry. The site’s certifications and diamond catalog pages are intended to show how laboratory reports, origin references, and compliance files can support buyer confidence.

Do you offer bespoke or custom jewelry manufacturing?

Yes. Cox Walker Jewelry LLC supports private commissions, custom settings, and refined jewelry manufacturing workflows. Clients can begin through the products, jewelry manufacturing, or contact pages depending on the scope of the request.

How does traceability work across your supply chain?

Our traceability page explains the path from mining through sorting, grading, cutting, crafting, and delivery. The goal is to make origin, handling, and process transparency easier for clients and trade partners to understand.

Are investor or partnership conversations welcome?

Yes. The investors page is structured to support high-level business review, and the contact page can route partnership, investor, and corporate enquiries to the appropriate team.

How can I stay informed about auctions, collections, or company news?

Visitors can follow the auctions and news pages for current editorial examples and use the contact page to request updates, catalogue support, or private notifications for specific areas of interest.
