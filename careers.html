Careers

Join a team spanning sourcing, operations, and refined presentation.

Careers at Cox Walker Jewelry LLC extend across field operations, technical coordination, client experience, and jewelry craftsmanship. This page is designed to communicate not only open roles, but also the level of care, professionalism, and shared ambition expected across every part of the business.

We are building a team that values integrity, precision, curiosity, and calm execution. Whether the role sits in the field, in the workshop, or close to clients, every position is expected to contribute to a culture of excellence and long-term trust.

Accra / Field

Operations Coordinator

Support logistics, scheduling, reporting, and communication across mining, transport, and plant teams to keep daily operations coordinated and accountable.

The role suits someone organized, dependable, and comfortable working across moving priorities without losing attention to detail.

Remote / New York

Private Client Advisor

Guide collectors and trade buyers through product selection, bespoke enquiries, presentation materials, and purchasing conversations with clarity and discretion.

This position is ideal for someone who understands luxury service, communicates with confidence, and can translate complex inventory into a calm client experience.

Workshop

Jewelry Production Specialist

Assist with finishing, quality review, atelier scheduling, and production planning to ensure pieces move efficiently from bench completion to final presentation.

Candidates should be comfortable with meticulous standards, collaborative problem-solving, and the quiet discipline of premium craftsmanship.
